PART 4: PLAYER DEVELOPMENT FRAMEWORK

Comprehensive assessment and position-flexible skill development

Comprehensive Player Assessment

Position-specific training matching natural abilities accelerates development by 40%

Physical Evaluation

  • Athleticism: Speed, jumping ability, lateral quickness
  • Coordination: Ball handling under pressure, ambidextrous skills
  • Growth Projection: Current vs projected adult size
  • Injury Profile: Previous injuries affecting development

Play Style Classification

  • Shooter: Catch-and-shoot, movement shooting emphasis
  • Slasher/Driver: Rim finishing, foul drawing focus
  • Facilitator: Decision-making, assist-to-turnover optimization
  • Scorer/Creator: Individual offense, shot creation development
  • Defender: Coverage versatility, communication leadership
  • Post Player: Interior skills, rebounding specialization

Mental Profile

  • Basketball IQ: Decision-making speed and accuracy
  • Competitiveness: Pressure and adversity response
  • Learning Style: Visual, auditory, or kinesthetic preference
  • Leadership Potential: Natural teammate influence
